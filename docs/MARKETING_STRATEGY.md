## 🏆 Top 3 Acquisition Targets (Hunting List)

### 1. Ledger Enterprise (France/Global)
- **Why:** They already have "Ledger Extension" and a massive enterprise client base. SentinelCortex is the perfect "Sovereign Layer" for their custom white-label solutions.
- **Value Prop:** "Military-grade PQC isolation for custom Enterprise hardware without compromising the core Ledger OS security."

### 2. Trezor Enterprise / SatoshiLabs (Czech Republic)
- **Why:** Trezor (Model T/Safe) is open-source, but many institutional clients (banks, gov) ask for a "Closed/Hardened" elite shell. SentinelCortex fills this gap as a premium B2B add-on.
- **Value Prop:** "Deterministic A-Code protection for Trezor-based institutional custodians."

### 3. SecuX / B2B Shield Division (Asia/Taiwan)
- **Why:** SecuX is aggressively moving into payment terminals and cold storage for large-scale institutional assets. They need a technology edge to outperform OneKey/Tangem in the European/US defense market.
- **Value Prop:** "Anti-Tamper & Key Shredding as a standard for high-security payment infrastructure."
